        <h1>About myself</h1>
        <p>I am currently a PhD candidate in Computer Vision at the Laboratory of Computer Science of the University of Tours under the supervision of 
          <a href="https://julien-mille.github.io/">Julien MILLE</a>, Nicolas RAGOT, Donatello CONTE and Dominique LI.
          My PhD subject is mainly about video analysis. In my research, I am interested in analyzing dynamic textures data. 
          Prior to that, I was an engineer student at INSA Centre Val de Loire.
          During my universtity years (2015-2018), I have done my internships at the Laboratory of Signal and Image Processing (Rennes) and in the company of RF-Track. 
          The domains that I have done during these two internships are mainly Embedded Software, Signal Processing and Machine Learning. </p>
        <h2>Experiences</h2>
        <h3>Signal Processing and Machine  Learning Internship (Mars - August 2018)</h3>
        <p>From Mars to August 2018, I have passed my internship in RF-Track under the supervision of M. Michel Le Mer. 
          The objectives of my internship are:</p>
        <ul>
          <li>To create algorithms by applying machine learning to classify animal activities.</li>
          <li>To implement these algorithms in a microcontroller in order to evaluate their performances.</li>
          <li>Create an automatic tool for future usage.</li>
        </ul>
        <h3>Computer Vision Final Year Project (September 2018 - January 2018)</h3>
        <p>In my last year at INSA Centre Val de Loire, I have done a final year project under the suprvision of M. Julien Mille. 
          The project is mainly about:</p>
        <ul>
          <li>Image processing: applying gaussian filters in smoothing images.</li>
          <li>Computer vision: image classification (LeNet) and image segmentation (U-Net) using deep learning.</li>
          <li>Programming language: Python.</li>
          <li>Libraries used: PyTorch, OpenCV, Pillow, etc.</li>
        </ul>

        <h3>Elderly Fall Detection Internship (April - August 2017)</h3>
        <p>During this internship, I was working under the supervision of M. Majd Saleh and Mrs. Régine Le Bouquin-Jeannès.

          The objectifs of the internship are :</p>
